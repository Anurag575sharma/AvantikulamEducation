About The School

History

Take A Look at How We Begin

Avantikulam Education started during the COVID-19 pandemic as an initiative to provide free education to underprivileged students. What began with a few passionate NIT and IIT students quickly grew into a community-driven movement, aiming to help students excel in competitive exams like JEE and school academics, bridging the gap in access to quality education!

Learn More

We are Avantikulam focused on Education and Enlightenment.

Born out of the challenges of the pandemic, our mission is to provide free, accessible learning resources and support to students who need it most!

Our passionate tutors, consisting mainly of NIT and IIT students, work tirelessly to help students achieve their academic goals, especially in competitive exams like JEE, while fostering a community of growth and opportunity.

Why Avantikulam Education?

At Avantikulam Education, we focus not only on imparting knowledge but also on building confidence, critical thinking, and problem-solving skills.

Free of Cost

We believe education should be accessible to all. Our programs are entirely free, supporting students from diverse backgrounds.

Dedicated Tutors

Classes are led by passionate tutors, including students from prestigious institutions like NITs and IITs, who bring real-world insights and competitive exam strategies.

Personalized Learning

We understand each student’s unique needs, providing tailored support to foster individual growth and success.

Focused on Academic Excellence

Our programs are designed to help students excel in exams like JEE and school academics, paving the way for future achievements.

Holistic Development

Beyond academics, we encourage confidence, critical thinking, and mental well-being to prepare students for life’s challenges.
